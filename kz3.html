eagle

Eagle (scientific name: Aquila), also known as kite, is a small raptor, and general birds, the female body is often larger than the male. Eagle ferocious temperament, the mouth was yellow, curved mouth, feet strong and powerful, toes have sharp claws, wing Dashan Fei.

parrot

Parrot is a parrot (scientific name: Psittaciformes) many beautiful feathers, birds love to call. Typical climbing birds, toe-shaped feet, two toes forward two toes backward, suitable for grasping, bird beak strong, you can eat hard fruit. Plum color bright, often as pets.

Magpie
